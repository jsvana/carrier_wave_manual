The Activity Feed shows notable achievements from you and your friends.

## Feed Content

The feed displays activities like:

- **New DXCC entities** - First contact with a new country
- **Band milestones** - 100 QSOs on a band, new band worked
- **Mode milestones** - First CW contact, FT8 milestones
- **Streaks** - Logging streak achievements
- **POTA** - Activations completed, parks worked milestones
- **Challenge progress** - Challenge completions, rank achievements

## Feed Filters

Filter the feed by:

- **All** - Everything from everyone
- **Friends** - Only people you follow
- **Clubs** - Activity from club members
- **Mine** - Only your achievements

## Achievement Detection

Carrier Wave automatically detects achievements by analyzing your log:

1. New QSO logged
2. Background analysis runs
3. Notable achievements identified
4. Added to your feed

No manual tracking required.

## Sharing

Share achievements to:

- Generate a share card image
- Post to social media
- Copy link to achievement

Share cards include:
- Achievement details
- Your callsign
- Carrier Wave branding

## Following

Connect with other Carrier Wave users:

1. Search by callsign
2. Tap **Follow**
3. See their activity in your feed

Following is one-way (they don't need to follow back).

## Privacy

Control what others see:

- **Public** - Anyone can see your activity
- **Friends only** - Only people you follow back
- **Private** - Your activity hidden from others

Configure in Settings.

## Clubs

Join clubs to see activity from club members:

- Club activity appears in your feed
- Contribute to club statistics
- Participate in club challenges
